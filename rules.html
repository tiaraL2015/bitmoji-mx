CONTROLS

Use the left and right arrow keys to move your player front and back. Use the space bar to jump.

Level 1:


Collect 20 points in level 1 while avoiding obstacles.

Level 2:


Collect 40 points in level 2 while avoiding obstacles and as the game speed increase.

Level 3:


Collect 60 points in level 2 while avoiding both obstacles and as the game speed increases.

Boss Level:


Avoid the fireballs for 90 seconds.

Multiplayer:


Compete against your opponent for the highest score.
